# Privacy Policy

**Effective Date:** April 17, 2025

## 1. Introduction
GTTM Team ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how **LingoLens** ("the App") collects, uses, and safeguards your information. By using the App, you agree to the terms of this policy.

## 2. Information We Collect

### A. Information You Provide
- **User Content:** Words, phrases, and translations you save or customize.
- **Learning Data:** Progress, quiz scores, and SRS (Spaced Repetition System) statistics.
- **Support Communication:** Any information you provide when contacting us for support.

### B. Automatically Collected Information
- **Usage Data:** We collect information on how you interact with the App (features used, time spent).
- **Device Information:** Device model, OS version, unique identifiers, and crash logs.

### C. Device Permissions
To provide its core features, LingoLens requires the following permissions:
- **Camera:** For real-time OCR and image translation.
- **Microphone:** For speech-to-text and pronunciation practice.
- **Storage/Media:** (Optional) To process saved images for translation.
- **Internet:** To access AI services and translation APIs.

## 3. Third-Party Services & SDKs
We use several trusted third-party services to enhance your experience. These services may collect data according to their own privacy policies:

- **Google ML Kit:** For on-device text recognition (OCR).
- **Google Mobile Ads (AdMob):** To display advertisements.
- **Firebase (Analytics & Crashlytics):** To monitor app performance and user engagement.
- **Google Play Services:** For app functionality and updates.
- **Cloud AI Providers:** (e.g., GPT-4o, Gemini) to provide advanced linguistic explanations and translations.
- **Open Source Libraries:** Including Coil (image loading), Lottie (animations), and Room (local database).

## 4. How We Use Your Data
- To provide and maintain the App's features.
- To personalize your learning journey and SRS schedules.
- To improve our AI models and app performance.
- To display relevant advertisements (in the free version).
- To comply with legal obligations.

## 5. Data Security & Storage
Most of your learning data is stored **locally on your device** using the Room database. We implement industry-standard security measures to protect any data transmitted to our cloud services.

## 6. GDPR & User Rights (For EEA Users)
If you are located in the European Economic Area (EEA), you have the following rights:
- **Right to Access:** Request a copy of your data.
- **Right to Erasure:** Request the deletion of your data.
- **Right to Portability:** Request transfer of your data.
- **Right to Withdraw Consent:** Manage your ad preferences and data collection settings within the App.

## 7. Children's Privacy
LingoLens does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we discover such data has been collected, we will delete it immediately.

## 8. Changes to This Policy
We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new policy within the App.

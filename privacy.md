# Privacy Policy

## Introduction
GTTM Team ("we," "our," or "us") built the LingoLens app ("Service," "app," "our app") to provide users with advanced language translation and learning tools. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and share information about you when you use our app.

By downloading, installing, or using LingoLens, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree with our policies and practices, please do not use our Service.

## Information We Collect

### Information You Provide
- **User-Created Content**: Words, phrases, and translations you create, save, or customize
- **Learning Progress**: Quiz scores, practice history, and learning statistics
- **Custom Settings**: Language preferences, UI customizations, and app settings

### Automatically Collected Information
- **Usage Data**: How you interact with the app, features used, time spent, and actions taken
- **Device Information**: Device type, operating system version, unique device identifiers
- **Log Data**: In case of errors, we collect data such as your device IP address, device name, operating system version, app configuration, time and date of use, and other statistics

### Permissions We Request
- **Camera**: Used only for OCR (Optical Character Recognition) text extraction from images
- **Microphone**: Used only for speech recognition and voice-to-text translation features
- **Internet Access**: Required for translation services, dictionary lookups, and app functionality
- **Network State**: To determine connectivity and provide offline features when appropriate
- **Notification Access**: To provide important updates and information about the service

## How We Use Your Information
We use the information we collect to:
- Provide and maintain our Service
- Improve and personalize your experience
- Develop new features based on how the app is used
- Monitor app performance and fix technical issues
- Communicate with you about the app

## Data Sharing and Disclosure
LingoLens respects your privacy. We do not sell or rent your personal data to third parties. However, we may share information in the following circumstances:

- **Third-Party Service Providers**: We work with companies that provide services on our behalf, such as:
  - Translation APIs for language translation
  - OCR services for text recognition from images
  - Voice recognition services for speech-to-text features
  - Analytics providers to understand app usage
  - Advertising partners for app monetization

- **Legal Requirements**: We may disclose your information if required by law, regulation, legal process, or governmental request.

## Third-Party Services

These third-party services have their own privacy policies addressing how they use such information.

## Advertising
LingoLens may display advertisements provided by third-party advertising networks. These networks may use cookies and similar technologies to collect information about your device and your app usage to provide targeted advertisements.

## Data Security
We value your trust and implement appropriate technical and organizational security measures to protect your personal information against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction.

## Children's Privacy
LingoLens does not address anyone under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children under 13. If we discover that a child under 13 has provided us with personal information, we immediately delete this information from our servers. If you are a parent or guardian and you are aware that your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us so that we can take necessary actions.

## Changes to This Privacy Policy
We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "effective date" at the top of this Privacy Policy. We may also notify you through in-app messages or other reasonable means.

You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes. Changes to this Privacy Policy are effective when they are posted on this page.
